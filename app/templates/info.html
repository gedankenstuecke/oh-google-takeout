Google Location History

This project adds your Google Location History to OpenHumans and allows you to visualize the same on a heatmap.

Log in to get started!

FAQ

Will my data be public?

The short answer: No.
The longer answer: No, unless you decide that you want to make this data public.
If that is the case then you can head over to Open Humans and make the full archive data publicly available.

How it works

  1. Login or create an account

    You can upload your data for Google Location History once you have done this.

  2. Authorize app

    This authorizes us to deposit your archive into your Open Humans account.

  3. Upload the data

    You will be redirected back to Google Location History and can then upload your data.
    You will be able to access & manage your data on Open Humans.
